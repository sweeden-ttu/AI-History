Getting Started

Your roadmap to making money with AI

Step 1: Learn the Tools

Start by mastering popular AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, and image generators. Understand their capabilities and limitations.

Step 2: Choose Your Niche

Select a specific area to focus on - writing, design, development, or consulting. Specialization leads to higher rates.

Step 3: Build Your Portfolio

Create sample work showcasing your AI abilities. Use these to attract your first clients.

Step 4: Find Clients

Use platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, LinkedIn, or direct outreach to find clients needing AI services.

Step 5: Scale Up

Increase rates as you gain experience. Create products for passive income. Build systems for efficiency.

Step 6: Diversify

Multiple income streams reduce risk. Combine freelancing, products, and passive income.
