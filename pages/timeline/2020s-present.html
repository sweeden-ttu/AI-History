2020s-Present: Generative AI Era

The age of transformers, GPT, and multimodal AI systems

2020: GPT-3

OpenAI releases GPT-3 with 175 billion parameters, demonstrating unprecedented language generation capabilities.

2021: DALL-E & GitHub Copilot

OpenAI releases DALL-E for image generation. GitHub Copilot brings AI coding assistance to developers.

2022: ChatGPT

ChatGPT launches and reaches 100 million users faster than any technology in history, sparking the AI revolution.

2022: Stable Diffusion

Open-source image generation models democratize AI art creation.

2023: GPT-4 & Multimodal AI

OpenAI releases GPT-4 with multimodal capabilities. Google launches Bard, Microsoft integrates AI across products.

2024: Claude, Gemini, and Open Models

Anthropic's Claude, Google's Gemini, and open-source models like Llama 2 push AI capabilities further.

2025-2026: Agentic AI

AI agents that can take actions, autonomous systems, and reasoning models emerge. AI becomes integrated into everyday tools.
