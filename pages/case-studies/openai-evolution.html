The Rise of Generative AI

The OpenAI Story

OpenAI transformed from a nonprofit AI research lab into one of the most influential tech companies, pioneering generative AI and bringing it to mainstream consumers.

Key Milestones

2015: Founded

Elon Musk, Sam Altman, and others commit $1 billion to OpenAI as a nonprofit AI research lab.

2018: GPT-1

OpenAI introduces Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT), a new approach to language models.

2020: GPT-3

GPT-3 with 175 billion parameters demonstrates unprecedented language generation, sparking the LLM revolution.

2022: ChatGPT

ChatGPT launches and reaches 100 million users in just 2 months - the fastest-growing app in history.

2023: Microsoft Partnership

Microsoft invests $10 billion in OpenAI, integrating GPT into Bing, Office, and Azure.

2024: Sora & GPT-4o

OpenAI releases Sora (video generation) and GPT-4o (multimodal), pushing AI boundaries further.
