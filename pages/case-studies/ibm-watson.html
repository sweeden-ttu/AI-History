IBM Watson

From Jeopardy Champion to Healthcare AI

The Watson Journey

IBM Watson represents one of the most ambitious AI projects in history, demonstrating both the promise and challenges of artificial intelligence in enterprise.

Key Milestones

2011: Jeopardy Victory

Watson defeats Ken Jennings and Brad Rutter on Jeopardy!, demonstrating advanced natural language processing and question answering capabilities.

2013: Healthcare Push

IBM announces Watson for Oncology, partnering with cancer hospitals to help diagnose and treat cancer patients.

2015-2016: Expansion

Watson expands into finance, weather, IoT, and more. IBM invests heavily in Watson Health.

2022: Sale to Max

IBM sells Watson Health to Max Health for $1 billion, marking the end of an era but also new opportunities.

Lessons Learned

  • AI requires clean data: Watson struggled with inconsistent medical data
  • Expertise matters: Domain experts are essential for AI success
  • Expectations vs reality: AI in healthcare is harder than expected
  • Persistence pays: IBM continues AI research despite setbacks
