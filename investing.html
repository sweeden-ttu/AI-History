Key Investment Patterns

Historical patterns that repeat in AI evolution

Hardware Cycles

AI breakthroughs follow hardware advances. GPU evolution (NVIDIA) drives AI capability leaps.

  • ✓ GPU architecture advances
  • ✓ Compute capacity increases
  • ✓ Cost per computation drops

Algorithm Evolution

New architectures (CNNs, Transformers) open new possibilities and investment opportunities.

  • ✓ Research breakthroughs
  • ✓ Open source releases
  • ✓ Commercial adoption

Business Model Shifts

From enterprise to consumer, from API to subscription. Watch for the next shift.

  • ✓ Pricing model changes
  • ✓ Distribution channels
  • ✓ Target markets expand

Infrastructure Build

Major AI waves are preceded by infrastructure investment. Data centers, cloud, networks.

  • ✓ Cloud capacity growth
  • ✓ Network bandwidth increases
  • ✓ Storage costs decline

Investment Approaches

Different ways to invest in AI's future

Direct Stocks

Buy shares in leading AI companies. NVIDIA, Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Meta.

Pros: High potential returns, full exposure

Cons: High risk, requires research

ETFs & Funds

AI-focused exchange-traded funds for diversified exposure.

Pros: Diversification, lower risk

Cons: Lower potential returns, fees

Hardware Plays

Focus on companies supplying AI infrastructure. Chips, data centers, networking.

Pros: Essential to AI growth

Cons: Cyclical, competitive

Emerging Players

Smaller AI companies and startups with high growth potential.

Pros: Highest upside

Cons: High risk, harder to analyze
