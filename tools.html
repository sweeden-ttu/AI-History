Getting Started with AI Tools

Tips for beginners

Start with Free Tools

Begin with free versions of popular AI tools. ChatGPT, Claude, and many others offer free tiers with generous limits.

Learn Prompt Engineering

The quality of AI output depends on your prompts. Learn to write clear, specific instructions for better results.

Combine Multiple Tools

Use different tools for different tasks. Each AI excels at different things. Experiment to find the best combination.

Stay Updated

AI evolves rapidly. Follow AI news and updates to stay on top of new tools and capabilities.
