ELIZA chatbot, expert systems, backpropagation algorithm, personal computers emerge.

Deep Learning

IBM Deep Blue beats Kasparov, AlexNet sparks deep learning revolution, AlphaGo defeats Go champion.

GPT-3, ChatGPT, DALL-E, Claude, Gemini. AI creates text, images, code, and more.
